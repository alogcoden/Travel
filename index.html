Osaka

Osaka is a bustling metropolis in Japan, celebrated for its unique blend of modernity and tradition. It's home to iconic attractions such as Osaka Castle, Universal Studios Japan, and the lively Dotonbori district. Known for its exceptional street food, including takoyaki and okonomiyaki, Osaka offers a rich cultural experience and vibrant nightlife.

Shanghai

Shanghai is a dynamic city in China, renowned for its impressive skyline featuring the Oriental Pearl Tower and the Shanghai Tower. It blends historical landmarks like The Bund with modern developments in Pudong. Known for its vibrant shopping districts, diverse cuisine, and cultural attractions, Shanghai stands as a global financial hub.

Seoul

Seoul is the bustling capital of South Korea, known for its blend of traditional and modern influences. The city features historic sites like Gyeongbokgung Palace and contemporary landmarks such as the N Seoul Tower. Renowned for its vibrant shopping districts, street food, and rich cultural heritage, Seoul is a dynamic global city.

Berlin

Berlin, the capital of Germany, is a city rich in history and culture. Known for its iconic landmarks such as the Brandenburg Gate and Berlin Wall, it also boasts a vibrant arts scene and diverse neighborhoods. Berlin offers a mix of historical sites, modern architecture, and a thriving nightlife, making it a dynamic European metropolis.
